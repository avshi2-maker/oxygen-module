Standard Session Parameters

📊 Session Structure

Standard Protocol:
  • Pressure: 2.0 ATA
  • Session time: 90 minutes
  • Air breaks: 3x 5-minute intervals
  • Compression: 10 minutes
  • Treatment: 60 minutes
  • Decompression: 10 minutes
Monitoring During Session:
  • Blood pressure checks (pre/post)
  • Oxygen saturation monitoring
  • Ear pressure equalization
  • Temperature regulation
  • Comfort level assessment

⚠️ Contraindications

  • Untreated pneumothorax
  • Recent thoracic surgery
  • Upper respiratory infection
  • Fever above 38.5°C
  • Claustrophobia (relative)
  • Seizure disorders (assess risk)

✅ Safety Checklist

  • No flammable materials
  • Remove all petroleum products
  • No electronic devices
  • 100% cotton clothing
  • Clear sinuses before treatment
  • Emergency protocols reviewed
